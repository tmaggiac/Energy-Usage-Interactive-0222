Energy mix by country
The world depends on electricity. How this vital resource is produced differs by each country and it's available infrastructure. Even though fossil fuels like oil, gas, and coal have been in decline for decades, they still had a strong hold on the majority of countries in 2015 as it's cheaper and widely available. Renewable energy was produced by the second highest number of countries, followed by hydroelectirc. Only eighteen total countries produced any with nuclear energy.
Source: World Bank (2015)
